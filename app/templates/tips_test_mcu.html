要求配置MCU的产品类型为：

Dimmer Switch(No-Neutral)：

  • Dimmer Switch(0x37)
  • Dimmer Switch(Premium)(0x38)

Switch(No-Neutral)：

  • Switch Toggle(0x3A)
  • Switch Paddle(0x39)
  • Switch Centre Button(0x3B)

Dimmer Switch

  • Dimmer Switch(0x30)
  • Dimmer Switch(Premium)(0x31)

Switch

  • Switch Toggle(0x34)
  • Switch Paddle(0x33)
  • Switch Centre Button(0x35)
